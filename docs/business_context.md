# Business Context

Many organizations rely on human reviewers to verify applications against predefined conditions.

Applications are typically classified into:

- Matching Conditions
- Non-Matching Conditions
- Conditions Requiring Human Confirmation

When a reviewer rejects a condition, they provide a free-text explanation.

Over time, these rejection reasons become difficult to analyze manually.

This project demonstrates how Large Language Models (LLMs) can transform those rejection reasons into structured business insights.
